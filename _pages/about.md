<span class='anchor' id='about-me'></span>

I am now a Postdoc at [AIoT Group](https://air.tsinghua.edu.cn/en/Research1/AI_IoT.htm), led by [Prof. Yunxin Liu](https://yunxinliu.github.io/), the Institute for AI Industry Research (AIR), Tsinghua University, China.
I also work very closely with [Prof. Ting Cao](https://tingcao952.github.io/), [Prof. Yuanchun Li](https://yuanchun-li.github.io/). Before that, I received dual PhD degress from the University of Göttingen, Germany, co-advised by [Prof. Xiaoming Fu](https://user.informatik.uni-goettingen.de/~fu/) and [Prof. Marcus Baum](https://scholar.google.de/citations?user=5SVQD3oAAAAJ&hl=de) and Nanjing University, co-advised by [Prof. Guihai Chen](https://cs.nju.edu.cn/gchen/) and [Prof. Haipeng Dai](https://cs.nju.edu.cn/daihp/).

My current research interest is emboided AI, especially memory/reasoning-powered VLA model and efficient VLA inference/training system.

My research has been published in top-tier venues including NSDI, EuroSys, ISCA, MobiSys, INFOCOM, and ACL, and journals including TON, TMC, TC, and IJCV. I have served on the program committees of ICDCS, DASFAA, and ICPADS. I am a member of ACM and IEEE.
