Biography

I am now a Research Fellow of AIoT Group, leading by Yunxin Liu, at Institute for AI Industry Research (AIR), Tsinghua University, China.

My research interests include deep learning system and algorithm design, Hardware/Software co-design. Currently I am focusing on efficient large language models serving on edge and empowering traditional systems, such as video analytics, by large generative models.

I received my dual PhDs respectively from University of Göttingen, Germany, co-advised by Xiaoming Fu and Marcus Baum, and Nanjing University, China, co-advised by Guihai Chen and Haipeng Dai. My doctorial dissertation of Göttingen awarded "summa cum laude" (Top 1% of PhD graduates@UGöttingen, Germany). After that, I had been working in the University of Göttingen as a researcher from 2022 to 2023, before join Tsinghua.

My research achievements are published in top-tier computer science conferences, such as NSDI, EuroSys, INFOCOM, and ACL, and top-tier computer science journals, such as TON, TMC, TC. I have also served as PC in conferences including ICDCS, DASFAA, ICPADS. I have got a range of awards, such as the Shuimu Tsinghua Scholar Program from Tsinghua University, and the Postdoctoral Talent Introduction Program from Ministry of Education, China. I am a member of ACM and IEEE.
