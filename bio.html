Biography

Weijun Wang is currently a Postdoc researcher in AIoT Group (leading by Yunxin Liu) from Institute for AI Industry Research, Tsinghua University, China. His research area is machine learning system, particularly video analytics system and DNN acceleration on mobile platform. He received dual Ph.D. degrees respectively from Nanjing University, China (co-advised by Guihai Chen and Haipeng Dai) and University of Göttingen, Germany (advised by Xiaoming Fu). He was a Researcher at the University of Göttingen from 2022 to 2023. His doctorial dissertation of Göttingen awarded "summa cum laude" (Top 1-5% of PhD graduates@UGöttingen, Germany). He was elected in the Shuimu Tsinghua Scholar Program (水木学者) from Tsinghua University, and the Postdoctoral Talent Introduction Program (国家博士后海外引才专项) from Ministry of Education, China. He is a member of ACM and IEEE.
